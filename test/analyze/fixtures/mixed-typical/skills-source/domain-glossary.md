# Domain Glossary

Business domain terminology and policy reference.

## Glossary

- **Tenant** — A paying business customer
- **Workspace** — A logical container within a tenant
- **Seat** — A user license

## Business Rules

- Each tenant may have multiple workspaces
- Domain policy: pricing terminology must align with the master glossary
- Policy ban: never refer to "users" as "accounts" — those are billing entities
